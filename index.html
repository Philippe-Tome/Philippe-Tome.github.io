Smart-Home-Hub

Fully open-source and privacy-friendly Home Automation Hub project to control and program everything possible in the home from one central place including temperature control, light control, smart-lock, automatically switching off the lights when no one is home, security camera taking a photo everytime someone comes close to your door and emailing it to you all of that without having to use any third party or cloud services .


Created with: Node-Red, Raspberry Pi 3b+, MQTT, Node.JS, Mosquitto, JavaScript, Pi camera, Pi touchscreen, ESP8266 Wi-Fi relays, Tasmota, ultrasonic sensor, temperaure and humidity sensor.
